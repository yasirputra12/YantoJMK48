Toyota AE86 Trueno - Drift King

Takumi Fujiwara's legendary AE86 Trueno captured in Initial D's iconic manga style. The panda Trueno drifting through the corners, demonstrating the perfect technique that made it the ghost of Mount Akina.

Mazda RX-7 FC - RedSuns Legend

The legendary Mazda RX-7 FC as immortalized in Initial D manga. Ryosuke Takahashi's white FC3S, leader of the RedSuns team, showcasing its iconic rotary power and perfect balance through the mountain passes.
